<span style="font-family: Impact;"><span class="bbc_center"><span style="font-size: 21px;">The Laser Reflector</span></span></span><br>

The redirected beam has the same properties like the source.<br>
You can mix diffrent laser beams and you can also put a lens on it.<br>

<span id="Color"></span><br>
<span class="bbc_center"><span style="font-size: 15px;"><strong class="bbc">COLORS</strong></span><br>
<img src="http://dl.dropbox.com/u/5339173/wecraftmodfinal/picture/img_improveLaser/img-dye.gif" alt="Posted Image" class="bbc_img"></span><br>
<br>
<strong class="bbc">White</strong><br>
 ?The detector beam. The beam is interruptible.<br>
<br>
<ul class="bbc"><li><em class="bbc">Power 1-3:</em> Blocked by items, mobs, and player. <br></li><li><em class="bbc">Power 4-6:</em> Blocked by mobs and players. <br></li><li><em class="bbc">Power 7-9:</em> Blocked by player only.</li></ul><br>

<br>
<strong class="bbc">Red</strong><br>
<span style="color: rgb(255, 0, 0);">?</span>The heat beam. Deals damage, sets the world on fire, destroys blocks. <br>
<br>
<ul class="bbc"><li><em class="bbc">Power:</em> Each level improves the damage per second, the change to set an entity on fire and the beam can dig through harder blocks. Maximum damage: 30 hearts per second!<br></li><li><em class="bbc">Advanced:</em> Prevents beam from destruction of items.</li></ul><br>
<br>
<strong class="bbc">Green</strong><br>

<span style="color: rgb(0, 128, 0);">?</span>The nature beam. Speeds up plant grow rate. If user cuts the tree segment which is under the effect of an advanced green beam, the whole tree will fall. This will allow for automatic tree farms.<br>
<br>
<ul class="bbc"><li><em class="bbc">Higher Power:</em> Speeds up plant grow rate.<br></li><li><em class="bbc">Advanced:</em> Tree-cutting mechanism avaible.</li></ul><br>
<br>
<strong class="bbc">Blue</strong><br>
<span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255);">?</span>The slow down beam. Slows down mobs on horizontal plane (e.g. they move slower, but jump as usual).<br>

<br>
<ul class="bbc"><li><em class="bbc">Power n:</em> Slows down target to n*10%.<br></li><li><em class="bbc">Advanced:</em> Slows down by vertical axis.</li></ul><br>
<br>
<strong class="bbc">Yellow</strong><br>
<span style="color: rgb(255, 165, 0);">?</span>The light beam. Has increased light level.<br>
<br>
<ul class="bbc"><li><em class="bbc">Power n:</em> 4+n Lightlevel<br></li><li><em class="bbc">Advancded:</em> The beam passes through solid blocks as if there wasn't any.</li></ul><br>

<br>
<strong class="bbc">Cyan</strong><br>
<span style="color: rgb(0, 191, 255);">?</span>The tractor beam. The beam pushes entities.  All sorts of farming machines as well as laser elevators are possible with this beam.<br>
<br>
<ul class="bbc"><li><em class="bbc">Power n:</em> Pulls stuff with acceleration 0.4*n m/s.<br></li><li><em class="bbc">Advanced:</em> Pushes instead. If the beam is horizontal, it also pushes entities top with same acceleration.</li></ul><br>
<br>
<strong class="bbc">Purple</strong><br>

 <span style="color: rgb(153, 50, 204);">?</span>The hybrid of red and blue.<br>
