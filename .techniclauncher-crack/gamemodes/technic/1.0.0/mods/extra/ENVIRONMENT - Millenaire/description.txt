Is your Minecraft world devoid of intelligent life?<br>Add
some randomly generated villages that live and build around you. Help
them expand their settlements via selling materials to them. For more
detailed information, read the enclosed Beginner's Guide PDF or
visit <a href="http://millenaire.wikia.com">http://millenaire.wikia.com</a><br><br>

<b>Key commands</b><br>
[V] - Display nearest villages<br>
[M] - Display current quest<br>
[CTRL+SHIFT+P] - Switch pathing method<br>(use this if Millenaire runs slow!)<br><br>

(NOTE: May cause performance issues on low-end systems!)
